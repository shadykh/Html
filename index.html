Welcome to Html Cheat Sheet

This is a demo site for a 03 Lap, we are the ( LOL ) team, Shady and Mousa, here will show you some html commands.

I will mention here the Styling and Emojis commands.

We will start with styling commands:
To add a color to the text:
<p> style="color:red;" I am Red</p>
The result is:

I am red


To change the font size:
<p> style="font-size:30px;"I am 30px</p>
The result is:

I am 30px

To change the text alignment:
<p>style="text-align:left"I am at the left</p>

I am at the left


To change the font family:
<p>style="font-family:courier;"I am the Courier.</p>

I am the Courier.
